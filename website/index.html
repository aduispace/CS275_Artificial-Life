Walking Bipedal

Simulation of Bipedal Robot by Computational Intelligence Algorithms

Outline

Platform

Open AI gym: a platform for reinforcement learning. It includes a growing collection of benchmark problems that expose a common interface, and a website where people can share their results and compare the performance of algorithms.

Algorithms

About Reinforcement Learning, it is a type of machine learning, and also a branch of artificial intelligence. It allows machines and software agents to determine the optimal behavior within a specific context to maximize its performance. And here a simple reward feedback is required for the agent to learn its behavior, which is known as the reinforcement signal.

Implementation

As mentioned before, we are building our project on the OpenAI platform and using the Box2D environment. The environment involves a bipedal walker and its physical model. This allows us to control the model through an well defined interface. The interaction between the physical model and the control system includes four parts.
